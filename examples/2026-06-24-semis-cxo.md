## 1. Tape (blind-scan conclusion)
- Narrow market: temperature 42 / sentiment 19 (cold) / valuation 64; HSTECH +1.3% but among ≥HK$10bn caps, decliners:advancers ≈ 3:1.
- Leadership tightly concentrated: semis (SMIC +8~9%, GigaDevice +5%, Montage +4%) and a fresh CXO/pharma rotation (WuXi names, Asymchem) topping the sector table.
- In one line: narrow, semis leading alone, broad weakness underneath → a **call tape**.

## 2. Two-way fish-positioning + money structure (core step)
| Name | Fish segment | Money structure (large/retail) | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| SMIC 981 | Call **mid** | Large +HK$1.97bn accumulating, retail net out | ✅ Core |
| WuXi Bio 2269 | Call **head** | Large +HK$0.6bn, healthy | ✅ Small taste |
| Zijin 2899 | Put mid (unconfirmed) | Large slightly out, medium buying the dip, held 29 | ⏳ Conditional |
| Hua Hong 1347 | (wrongly tagged tail by magnitude) | Large +HK$6.75bn **still accumulating** = stock is NOT a tail; but its warrants' IV is 90% = uneconomic | ❌ Excluded (economics gate) |
| BABA/Xiaomi/BYD | (wrongly tagged as puts) | Large **accumulating at the lows** = not puts; potential call heads | ❌ No puts |

> **Key lesson (no false kills)**: tails are judged by **structure + parabolic velocity, never by % move**. Hua Hong at all-time-high is NOT a tail (large still accumulating); it's excluded because of extreme IV (the **economics gate**, separate from the fish gate). BABA "fell a lot" ≠ a put (large is buying the bottom). A false kill = a hole in the framework.
